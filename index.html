About GTL GXNG

Hailing from Nairobi, Kenya, GTL GXNG is an emerging hip-hop/rap collective making waves in the UK music scene. The group features members including Deno, bringing a unique perspective shaped by their Kenyan roots. GTL GXNG blends contemporary UK rap with melodic elements and an authentic East African edge, creating a distinctive sound that stands out in the British hip-hop landscape.

The collective has been building momentum with a strong catalog of releases, working with talented artists like Lucky and LUCCI, showcasing versatility and a commitment to creating diverse musical experiences. Tracks like "LIFESTYLE - FREESTYLE," "Lit," "Kid Freestyle," "HBDAY," "POOKIE DUSKI," and "DUSKIE" demonstrate GTL GXNG's raw talent and authentic storytelling ability.

With a prolific output that includes multiple albums, EPs, and singles released in 2024 and 2025, GTL GXNG represents the new generation of UK rap artists who are shaping the sound of British hip-hop. Their music explores themes of urban life, relationships, and personal experiences, delivered with authentic energy and contemporary production.

From the introspective tracks on albums like "moon and stars" to the hard-hitting singles like "GENESIS" featuring LIFESTYLE - FREESTYLE, GTL GXNG continues to evolve as a collective, consistently dropping new music and building a dedicated fanbase across streaming platforms. Representing Nairobi on the global stage, GTL GXNG bridges African and UK musical influences, contributing to the increasingly diverse and international sound of British hip-hop.
